This game is to showcase the RECALL function that is part of the radix network. A resource with the recall trait activated can be retreived. For the C.O.W. the retreival conditions are set to allowAll, meaning everyone can recall the NFT from wherever on the network.

The Game: After a week of visiting all corners of the Radix Network... or not... the weeks wanderings of the C.O.W. will be posted. From the number of transactions the C.O.W. has been subject to, one will be chosen as the winning transaction.

A price of 1M $BALLS awaits the account that made the winning transaction.
